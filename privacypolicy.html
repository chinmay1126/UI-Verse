Privacy Policy

Transparent, simple, and user-first data policy. We respect your privacy and only collect what is necessary.

Last Updated: May 2026 GDPR Compliant

01

Introduction

UIverse is committed to protecting user privacy. This policy explains how we handle data and what we do to keep it secure.

By using UIverse, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you have questions, please reach out to our support team.

02

Data We Collect

We believe in data minimization and only collect what is necessary to provide you with the best experience.

Name & Email

Only collected if you explicitly provide them during registration or when contacting support.

Usage Analytics

Anonymized information about pages visited to help us understand user journeys and improve the platform.

Device Information

Basic technical details like browser type and operating system to ensure compatibility.

GDPR Ready

Privacy-first data practices

Encrypted

HTTPS & secure storage

Minimal Tracking

Only necessary analytics

Deletion Rights

Request removal anytime

03

How We Use Data

The data we collect is strictly used to maintain and improve our services.

  • To improve UI components and provide personalized recommendations.
  • To identify and fix bugs or system errors.
  • To analyze aggregate usage trends and optimize our infrastructure.
  • To communicate important updates, security alerts, and policy changes.
04

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and optional cookies for analytics. You can manage your preferences at any time.

Type Purpose Duration
Authentication Keeps you securely logged into your account. Session / 30 Days
Preferences Remembers your theme (light/dark) and UI settings. 1 Year
Analytics Helps us understand how the site is used (anonymized). 6 Months
05

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

While we use HTTPS encryption and secure servers, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

06

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to facilitate our operations. These services only have access to the data necessary to perform their specific tasks.

Google Analytics

Used for anonymized traffic analysis and understanding user demographics.

GitHub Hosting

Our code and static assets are securely hosted on GitHub infrastructure.

07

Your Rights

You have full control over your personal information. Under the GDPR and other privacy laws, you are entitled to:

  • Request a copy of your stored data.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Request complete deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of non-essential analytics tracking.
08

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
